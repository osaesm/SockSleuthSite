The Sock Sleuth

Amazon Bookshop.org Barnes & Noble

When a string of disappearing socks leaves Mom grumbling, Emma beckons the world's slowest detective. Cole Sloth - Investigator crawls onto the crime scene to crack the case. After a high-stakes (and very low-speed) interrogation of Old Willie washer, Ms. Dee Dee dryer, and Haggard Harry hamper, the sleuth catches the culprit red-handed: A pair of pants clinging to the socks to remember the fun and joy of Emma's bedtime dance. It's a whimsical race against the clock, though with Cole Sloth in charge, the "race" is more of a crawl that ends with a solved mystery and a sleepy detective.
