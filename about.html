About Francis Cleetus

Francis Cleetus is an award-winning illustrator based in Portland, Oregon. With a background in fine arts and children's literature, he has illustrated over twenty books for authors around the world. His work is known for its dreamy watercolor textures and whimsical characters that leap off the page.

Francis's illustrations have been featured in galleries across the United States, and he regularly conducts workshops for aspiring artists. When he's not drawing, you can find him hiking in the Pacific Northwest or reading fantasy novels with his cat, Mochi.
