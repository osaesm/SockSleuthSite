About the Writer

Christina Caragein

Christina Caragein is a mystery writer with a passion for quirky characters and tangled plots. She spent ten years as a journalist before turning to fiction, and her work has appeared in various literary magazines. Christina lives in Seattle with her two dogs and a collection of vintage typewriters. The Sock Sleuth is her debut children's book.

About the Illustrator

Francis Cleetus

Francis Cleetus is an award-winning illustrator based in Portland, Oregon. His whimsical watercolor style has brought to life over twenty books for children and young adults. When he's not drawing, Francis enjoys hiking and reading fantasy novels with his cat, Mochi. He is the illustrator of The Sock Sleuth.
